Postitions of Responsibilities

Senior Patrol Leader:
the senior patrol leader is the top scout elected by fellow scouts. He with the help of scoutmasters plans the scout meetings and campouts. He observes and helps the patrol leaders.

Assistant Senior Patrol Leader:
the assistant senior patrol leaders job is to take place of the senior patrol leader in his time of absence. also to take charge of all other responsibitlies

Patrol Leader: the patrol leader is in charge of taking charge of his patrol,one duty is to report information to patrol members. like reminding them of important days, and to encourge them to go on campouts and troop activities.

Assistant Patrol Leader: to take place of patrol leader in time of absence.

Other Responsibilities

Scribe: the take attendence and write notes at PLC.
Chaplain's Aid: to lead in prayer at meetings,campouts,meals,etc...
Librarian: to take pictures at campouts and keep troop books in order
Instructor: should have good speeking skills or be willing to learn them. your job is to teach the troop new skills, or introduce new things
Quatermaster: an important job of checking up on troop equipment, making sure everything is in good shape. and if not then to report damages to troop scoutmaster
