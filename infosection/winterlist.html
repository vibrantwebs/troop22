Winter Camping Packing List

(based on a regular weekend campout)

  • thick sleeping bag or mummy bag (or two thin sleeping bags)
  • thin sheet (keeps frost away from sleeping bag)
  • pillow
  • stocking cap (ballcaps don't count)
  • 2 thick coats
  • 2 to 3 sweat shirts
  • 3 to 4 long-long sleeve shirts
  • 2 pairs of sweatpants
  • 3 to 4 pairs of jeans
  • 5 to 7 pairs of socks (no ankle socks)
  • 4 to 5 pairs of underwear
  • 2 to 3 pairs of gloves
  • waterbottle
  • flashlight with batteries
  • pocket knife
  • mess kit with silverware
  • raincoat
  • toothpaste and toothbrush
