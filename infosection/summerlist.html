Summer Camping Packing List

(based on a regular weekend campout)

  • thin sleeping bag (or thin sheet)
  • pillow
  • ballcap
  • 3 to 4 tee shirts
  • 3 to 4 pairs of shorts
  • 1 to 2 pairs of jeans (for hiking)
  • 4 to 6 pairs of socks (no ankle socks)
  • 4 to 5 pairs of underwear
  • waterbottle
  • flashlight with batteries
  • pocket knife
  • mess kit with silverware
  • raincoat
  • sun glasses
  • sunscreen
  • bug spray
  • towel
  • soap
  • toothpaste and toothbrush
